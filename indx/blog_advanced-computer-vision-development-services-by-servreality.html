What is Computer Vision?

Computer vision is a field of artificial intelligence that trains computers to interpret and understand the visual world. By analyzing images and videos, machines can detect patterns, recognize objects, and make decisions based on the visual information they receive. This technology opens up endless possibilities for automation, quality control, and enhanced user experiences. Companies can leverage computer vision for facial recognition, automated surveillance, defect detection in manufacturing, medical imaging analysis, and much more.
