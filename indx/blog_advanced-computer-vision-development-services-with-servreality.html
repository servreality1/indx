What Is Computer Vision?

Computer vision is a field of artificial intelligence that enables machines to interpret and make decisions based on visual data from the world around them. Whether it’s recognizing objects in a video stream, analyzing medical images, or enhancing augmented reality experiences, computer vision transforms how industries operate. By leveraging cutting-edge algorithms and deep learning, ServReality creates solutions that bring this technology to life.

Interesting Facts about Computer Vision

Did you know that the concept of computer vision dates back to the 1960s, when researchers first began experimenting with pattern recognition? Since then, it has grown into a vast field encompassing various techniques and applications.
